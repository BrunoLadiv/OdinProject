Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

Strawberry ice cream is a refreshing and fruity frozen treat. Made with fresh strawberries, cream, and a touch of sweetness, it's perfect for hot summer days or whenever you're craving something cool and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Step 1: In a blender or food processor, puree the strawberries until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Step 2: In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Step 3: Stir in the strawberry puree until well combined.
  4. Step 4: Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until thoroughly chilled.
  5. Step 5: Once chilled, pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Step 6: Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for an additional 2 to 4 hours, or until firm.
  7. Step 7: Serve the strawberry ice cream in bowls or cones, and enjoy!
